Introduction to TLE Components

The first week of August marked the beginning of our Technology and Livelihood Education journey. We were introduced to the various components of TLE, including ICT, Entrepreneurship, and Home Economics.

Our teacher explained how TLE prepares us for real-world challenges by combining theoretical knowledge with practical applications. This approach helps develop both technical skills and entrepreneurial mindset.

Activities Completed:

  • Orientation on TLE curriculum and objectives
  • Assessment of prior knowledge and skills
  • Introduction to basic computer operations
  • Team building activities for group projects
