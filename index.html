Taiwan Trip

August 22–25, 2025

Exploring Taipei and Ximending

From August 22 to 25, we traveled to Taiwan for a family trip. The weather was hot, but that didn't stop us from enjoying every moment of sightseeing, shopping, and exploring the city's famous attractions.

On August 22, we checked into our hotel at Midtown and then went shopping right after arrival. We explored Ximending, where we bought shoes and clothes to kick off the trip.

August 23 was one of the most exciting days as we visited the iconic Taipei 101 Observatory. The view from the 101st floor was breathtaking, and we took plenty of pictures. Riding the high-speed elevator was a fun experience, and afterward, we shopped at the Taipei 101 Mall. I even bought a pair of New Balance shoes and enjoyed a meal at McDonald's in the food court.

On August 24, we returned to Ximending for another round of shopping, mostly for new clothes. The lively atmosphere made the experience memorable.

Our final day, August 25, was spent shopping once again in Ximending. This time we bought clothes, shoes, souvenirs, and even a new pair of glasses before heading home. It was the perfect way to wrap up the trip.

Trip Highlights:

  • Stayed at Midtown Hotel
  • Shopping in Ximending for clothes, shoes, and souvenirs
  • Visited Taipei 101 Observatory (101st floor, high-speed elevator)
  • Bought New Balance shoes at Taipei 101 Mall
  • Ate McDonald's at the food court
  • Got new glasses before heading home

Buwan ng Wika

August 29, 2025

Celebrating Language, Culture, and Teamwork

On August 29, we celebrated Buwan ng Wika. I was part of the Blue Lions, and our team actively joined different events. We placed 1st in the Quiz Bee, which was one of our biggest achievements, and 4th place in the Sabayang Pagbigkas competition.

For the Sabayang Pagbigkas, we practiced the Kabataan Filipino poem for about two weeks. Although we didn't reach the top spot, the effort and teamwork we put in made the experience truly meaningful.

We also joined the exhibition, where each group represented a region of the Philippines. Our group showcased Bicol, and I contributed by helping prepare and set up the decorations as well as doing research. While we didn't win the championship, we proudly secured 3rd place.

Overall, the celebration was a success. Everyone gave their best, and it was a memorable experience that highlighted teamwork, culture, and school spirit.

Competition Results:

  • 1st Place in the Quiz Bee
  • 4th Place in Sabayang Pagbigkas – Kabataan Filipino
  • Represented Bicol region in the exhibition
  • 3rd Place overall in the exhibition showcase
  • Teamwork and cultural appreciation throughout the events
